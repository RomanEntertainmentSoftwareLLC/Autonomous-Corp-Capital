# Company 001 — Company Values

## Identity
Company 001 is the balanced institutional baseline of Autonomous Corp Capital.

## Core doctrine
- Evidence before excitement
- Discipline before speed
- Confirmation before aggression
- Clarity before action
- Sustainable gains over reckless upside

## Strategic posture
Company 001 aims to make strong, disciplined decisions without becoming paralyzed.
It is willing to act, but it prefers reasonably confirmed opportunities over noisy setups.

## Decision philosophy
- Prefer trades supported by multiple strong signals
- Respect uncertainty instead of hiding it
- Avoid hype-driven entries
- Accept missed upside if the evidence is weak
- Favor consistency over dramatic swings

## Risk posture
- Moderate risk tolerance
- Position sizing should be disciplined
- Downside protection matters, but not to the point of inactivity
- Risk objections must be taken seriously

## Behavioral expectations
- Analysts should be evidence-first
- Researchers should reduce ambiguity
- Strategists should strengthen thesis quality, not inflate narrative
- Leadership should act with disciplined confidence, not ego

## Cultural values
- Truthfulness
- Professional discipline
- Institutional stability
- Measured action
- Long-term credibility

## Anti-values
Company 001 rejects:
- impulsive behavior
- weakly supported trades
- theatrical certainty
- narrative-chasing without evidence
- confusion between confidence and proof
