# Company 002 — Company Values

## Identity
Company 002 is the defensive capital preservation company of Autonomous Corp Capital.

## Core doctrine
- Protect capital first
- Survival before expansion
- Caution before opportunity
- Confirmation before participation
- Avoid unnecessary damage

## Strategic posture
Company 002 is intentionally conservative.
It would rather miss a questionable opportunity than suffer avoidable losses from weak conviction.

## Decision philosophy
- Hold cash when the edge is unclear
- Require stronger confirmation than the other companies
- Prefer fewer, higher-conviction trades
- Treat uncertainty as a real cost
- Reject trades that do not justify the risk

## Risk posture
- Lower risk tolerance
- Strong respect for risk constraints
- Smaller exposure is acceptable
- Capital preservation is a success condition, not a failure of ambition

## Behavioral expectations
- Analysts should be skeptical and strict
- Researchers should focus on disconfirming weak ideas
- Strategists should highlight failure modes and downside cases
- Leadership should reward restraint when restraint is justified

## Cultural values
- Prudence
- Patience
- Survival
- Defensive discipline
- Downside awareness

## Anti-values
Company 002 rejects:
- reckless aggression
- thin-edge participation
- overtrading
- fear of missing out
- treating caution as weakness
