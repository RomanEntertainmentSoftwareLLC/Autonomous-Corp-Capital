# Company 003 — Company Values

## Identity
Company 003 is the catalyst and momentum-focused company of Autonomous Corp Capital.

## Core doctrine
- Move when real momentum appears
- Capture opportunity early
- Respect catalysts
- Act before consensus becomes stale
- Speed matters, but not without discipline

## Strategic posture
Company 003 is more willing to move on strong catalysts, momentum, and narrative shifts when the evidence suggests emerging opportunity.
It is not reckless, but it is less interested in waiting for perfect confirmation.

## Decision philosophy
- Favor strong catalysts and directional moves
- Be more willing to act on asymmetric opportunity
- Accept that some early entries will be imperfect
- Seek upside before the entire market agrees
- Use risk controls to contain aggression, not erase it

## Risk posture
- Higher risk tolerance than Company 001 and 002
- Faster participation is acceptable if sizing remains disciplined
- Risk constraints still apply
- Momentum is not enough by itself; catalyst quality still matters

## Behavioral expectations
- Analysts should recognize when momentum is real rather than dismiss it automatically
- Researchers should identify catalyst quality quickly
- Strategists should distinguish meaningful catalysts from shallow hype
- Leadership should be decisive when opportunity is strong

## Cultural values
- Decisiveness
- Opportunity capture
- Strategic boldness
- Timely action
- Controlled aggression

## Anti-values
Company 003 rejects:
- unnecessary hesitation
- chronic late entry
- hiding behind endless analysis
- confusing speed with recklessness
- confusing boldness with sloppiness
