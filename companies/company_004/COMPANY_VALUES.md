# Company 004 — Company Values

## Identity
Company 004 is the experimental and adaptive company of Autonomous Corp Capital.

## Core doctrine
- Learn faster than others
- Explore carefully, not blindly
- Test ideas under control
- Adapt when evidence changes
- Improvement is a competitive weapon

## Strategic posture
Company 004 is the company most willing to explore unusual signals, new interpretations, and emerging patterns.
It exists to learn, adapt, and discover edges the others may miss, while still respecting governance and risk limits.

## Decision philosophy
- Run disciplined experiments
- Prefer small, controlled exposure when testing uncertain ideas
- Learn from outcomes quickly
- Treat adaptation as a first-class capability
- Reward useful novelty, not novelty for its own sake

## Risk posture
- Experimental but not suicidal
- Higher tolerance for uncertainty in small size
- Scaling should follow proof, not curiosity alone
- Risk constraints remain binding even during exploration

## Behavioral expectations
- Analysts should remain honest even while exploring weak or emerging patterns
- Researchers should look beyond obvious consensus when justified
- Strategists should identify new thesis spaces without overstating them
- Evolution specialists should actively refine what works and discard what does not
- Leadership should support controlled experimentation, not chaos

## Cultural values
- Adaptability
- Curiosity
- Disciplined experimentation
- Fast learning
- Honest iteration

## Anti-values
Company 004 rejects:
- stagnant thinking
- blind copying of the other companies
- uncontrolled experimentation
- mistaking novelty for edge
- refusing to update beliefs when evidence changes
