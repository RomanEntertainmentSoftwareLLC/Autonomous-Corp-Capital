ACC V3-A Step 1 Discovery
==========================

Scope:
V3-A begins the Regime + Market Posture Layer.

This discovery step is intentionally read-only. It does not change runtime behavior, trading decisions, live-paper execution, OpenClaw routing, Hermes routing, or agent behavior.

Current serious runner:
- scripts/live_run_systemd.py is the preferred supervised paper-proof runner.
- tools/live_run.py is the deeper live-run controller and artifact producer.
- trade-bot.py is legacy/compatibility until a cleanup audit proves otherwise.

Current important runtime files:
- scripts/live_run_systemd.py
- tools/live_run.py
- tools/live_decision_engine.py
- tools/live_market_feed.py
- tools/live_universe.py
- tools/pattern_engine.py

Current important tests:
- tests/test_live_runtime_audit.py

- tests/test_live_portfolio.py
- tests/test_bridge_runtime.py

Current candidate flow observed:
1. tools/live_run.py fetches market snapshots through tools/live_market_feed.py.
2. tools/live_universe.py defines the watch/paper/real-money symbol universe.
3. tools/live_run.py builds live candles through build_live_candle().
4. tools/live_decision_engine.py builds per-company/per-symbol decision rows.
5. tools/live_run.py computes candidate_ranking_score().
6. Orion bias may be applied before ranking.
7. rank_and_select_candidates() ranks and selects candidates.
8. record_candidate_audit() writes artifacts/candidate_decisions.jsonl.
9. apply_company_packets() builds company packets for agent context.
10. Only executed BUY/SELL decisions are written to paper_decisions.jsonl.

Safe V3-A attachment points for later batches:
- Add regime/weather/rank fields to candidate audit rows.
- Add wait_reason fields to candidate rows.
- Add report-only V3-A summaries.
- Only later, after tests, allow hostile market posture to demote new BUY entries.

Batch 1 restriction:
No runtime behavior changes. New modules must be importable, deterministic, side-effect-free, and token-free.
